## PRODUCTION NOTES

### Voice Settings (ElevenLabs)
- **Voice**: Aria (warm, professional female) or Marcus (authoritative, friendly male)
- **Stability**: 0.6 (natural variation)
- **Similarity**: 0.8 (consistent character)
- **Style**: 0.4 (conversational, not robotic)
- **Speaker Boost**: ON

### Pacing
- Speak at 140-160 words per minute (conversational pace)
- Pause 0.5s after the hook
- Pause 0.3s between feature callouts
- Emphasize: "YOUR way", "YOUR language", "one-size-fits-YOU"

### Music Bed
- Subtle, modern, uplifting background music (low volume, -20dB)
- Build energy from hook to features
- Drop music slightly during voice to ensure clarity
- Swell at the closer

### Visual Sync Points
- 0:05 - Show Fathom logo/interface
- 0:15 - Show lens selection
- 0:25 - Show generation in action
- 0:35 - Show playback with features
- 0:50 - Show library/saved tracks
- 1:10 - End card with URL

## RECORDING TIPS

1. **Record in a quiet room** - No echo, no background noise
2. **Use a good mic** - Even a decent USB mic makes a huge difference
3. **Stand up while recording** - Better breath control and energy
4. **Smile while speaking** - It comes through in the voice
5. **Do 3-5 takes** - Pick the best one or composite the best parts
6. **Leave 2 seconds of silence** at start and end for editing
